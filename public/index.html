Welcome to the Id!otTest

Do you dare to play the famous brain teaser game? Here is how the IdiotTest works:

Once you click "Start the Quiz button" the timer will start and your questions will appear. This is a multiple choice quiz and there'll be 4 answer choices to every question. There is an answer to every question. The timer is called a Money Meter where you shall begin with a certain amount of money at the beginning of the question and that money will decrement a certain amount each second you do not answer the question. Once you reach to the Bonus question, if you answer it correctly you'll get $500 added to your score.

Note: the amount of money you start out with and the amount of money decreased each second will depend on the question (difficulty). Read the question and also look at the images carefully as they may contain hints to answer question.

If you click an answer before your Money Meter ran out, there are two possibilies. 1) If you got the correct answer, the money left on your meter meter is yours for that question. 2) If you didn't get the correct answer, you will have a score of $0 for that question. If you let your Money Meter run out and you haven't answered the question, you'll also get $0 for that question. When you've reached the Bonus question, if you answer the question wrong, you do not get an addiitonal $500 added to your money meter. However all the money you earned in the last 5 questions will be yours so all is not lost.

The goal is to try to answer the questions quickly to maximize the money you earn but avoid looking like an IDIOT.

Note the following for the scoring:

  • Difficulty 1 your Money Meter starts at $300 and decreases $20 every second.
  • Difficulty 2 your Money Meter starts at $500 and decreases $40 every second.
  • Difficulty 3 your Money Meter starts at $700 and decreases $60 every second.
  • Difficulty 4 your Money Meter starts at $900 and decrease $80 every second.
  • Difficulty 5 your Money Meter starts at $1100 and decrease $100 every second.
  • Bonus Question: starting timer starts at 10 seconds and you must answer question correctly within 10 seconds to win an additional $500.
